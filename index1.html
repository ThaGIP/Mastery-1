The Art of Deejaying R&B & Hip-Hop Music (1978-2025)

DJing has been the heartbeat of R&B and Hip-Hop music. From vinyl turntables to digital controllers, the art of mixing has evolved.

From Grandmaster Flash to DJ Jazzy Jeff to modern-day Serato DJs, the culture keeps growing.

Technology has revolutionized DJing.

Essential DJing Eras (1978-2025):

  1. The Vinyl Era (1978-1999)
  2. The Digital Revolution (2000-2010)
  3. The Controller Age (2011-2025)

Key DJ Techniques:

  • Scratching
  • Beatmatching
  • Sampling
  • Looping
